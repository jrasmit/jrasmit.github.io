About

Hey, I'm Jasper — 35 years old and living in the beautiful village of Itter in Tirol. I'm a certified Mountain Hiking and Trail Running Guide, passionate about helping people discover the outdoors safely and enjoyably. I speak Dutch, English, and German, so chances are we’ll understand each other just fine on the trail.

  • ✔️ Custom outdoor adventures and team-building events
  • ✔️ Safety workshops: navigation, weather, and mountain basics
  • ✔️ Multilingual guiding: Dutch, English, and German
