Trail Guide Jasper

Your Guide

Hey, I'm Jasper —

35 years old and living in the beautiful village of Itter in Tirol. I'm a certified Mountain Hiking Guide (Bergwanderführer) and Trail Running Guide, am an avid trail running and mountain explorer, and I'm passionate about helping people discover our beautiful mountains in Tirol, in a safe and enjoyable manner. I am fluent in German, Dutch, and English, and speak bits of French, Japanese and Spanish, making communication on the trails easy and fun!
