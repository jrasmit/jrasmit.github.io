About

Hey, I'm Jasper — 35 years old and living in the beautiful village of Itter in Tirol. I'm a certified Mountain Hiking and Trail Running Guide, passionate about helping people discover the outdoors safely and enjoyably. I speak German, Dutch, and English, making communication on the trails as easy as possible!

What I Do

  • ✔️ Guided mountain hikes for all levels, from families to experienced trekkers
  • ✔️ Trail running tours and technique coaching in the Alps
  • ✔️ Custom outdoor adventures and team-building events
  • ✔️ Safety workshops: navigation, weather, and mountain basics
  • ✔️ Multilingual guiding: Dutch, English, and German
